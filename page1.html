What is smart clothing?

Smart clothing is clothing that has technology embedded in it. Smart clothes also known as smart garments, and smart textiles are clothing items that use technology to increase their use beyond what the clothing is traditionally used for. When people think about smart clothing they think of fitbits, apple watches, and technology worn by those who need their health monitored; however smart clothing has expanded beyond just these devices. With the use of nanotechnology many companies are working to create smart garments. These smart clothing can sense and respond to temperature, heart rate, blood pressure, and more. Although not many companies are looking to make smart clothing because of its low demand as well as its cost. Smart clothing can go up to 4 times the usual price because of the technology that is used in it. This makes it harder for providers to sell to the average consumer. However, with the rise of technology, smart clothing is sure to be the next best thing on the market.

Examples of smart clothing

    Currently there are not many clothing companies investing in and making smart clothing, however, as of recently clothing companies are collaborating with tech companies to produce clothing meant for the future. Not only has smart clothing made it into fashion but it is also used in many other fields.

    Smart Medical Wearables:

  • Smart clothing is slowly making it into the medical field. Currently Edema ApS has developed a washable stocking that allows patients with edema (which is fluid swelling in the lower limbs) to measure and monitor the changes in their leg volume. This is much more patient friendly since it does not require them to be attached to machinery. Though this is not yet available to patients, it is being used in clinical trials.

    • Smart Activewear:

  • Smart devices have always been a part of sports, helping athletes and the average person reach their potential. Smart active wear has become more popular throughout the fashion industry. Currently Ralph Lauren has made t-shirts that connect to your smartphone app via bluetooth to record your fitness activity as well as recommend new workouts.

    • Hexoskin Smart Shirts:

  • Hexoskin is a Montreal based health company that specializes in manufacturing smart clothing. This company has developed high tech smart shirts that can monitor heart rate, breathing, activity intensity, steps, sleep and more. Not only are these shirts great for people who want to keep track of their vitals while exercising, but it is also great for people with medical needs. Hexoskin has been used by health researchers in clinical development in cardiology, pulmonolofy, neurology and more.
